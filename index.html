Hey, I’m Nick

Nick Jordan
October 17, 2025

Welcome to my little corner of the internet where I share the random things I’m into: personal projects, hobbies, food, travels, thoughts, opinions, and the occasional product review. It’s a mix, and that’s the fun of it.

Work in a nutshell

I’ve worked in marketing and performance analytics since 2015, after graduating from Eastern Michigan University with a dual major in finance and marketing. I spent years on the agency side and, in 2024, moved client-side to Ford Motor Company, focusing on campaign measurement. I love the blend of data, experimentation, and impact.

My general vibe

I’m a serial hobbyist who takes the 80/20 rule to the limit. I’m a proud jack-of-many-trades and a master of the stuff I do for work. I like learning, tinkering, and building systems that make life a little smoother.

This site is my home base for documenting, thinking out loud, and sharing what I learn. If it helps or entertains, cool. If not — it still glows green.
