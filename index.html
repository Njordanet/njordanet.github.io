  • Blog: long-form posts live at blog.njorda.net

About
Running and recreation. I log miles, chase race PRs, and generally use running as an excuse to explore places and clear my head.

Technology and techno. I grew up as a full-on consumer tech nerd and never really stopped. There’s usually a new gadget, app, or strange workflow on my desk. Somewhere in the background, there’s probably a beat looping.

I write things and take bad pictures and post them online. Essays, notes, and half-baked ideas tend to land on the blog or Substack. Photos, screenshots, and other ephemeral bits get scattered across the internet, but this page stays the home base.

If any of that sounds interesting, head over to the blog or find me on Bluesky.
